Provide Clean Drinking Water to Rural Villages

Organized by Sarah Jenkins verified

The Story

Access to clean water is a fundamental human right, yet thousands in the rural district of Manya still walk miles every day to collect water from unsafe sources. This campaign aims to change that.

We are raising funds to drill 5 new solar-powered boreholes. These sustainable water systems will provide clean, reliable drinking water to over 2,500 residents, drastically reducing waterborne illnesses and freeing up hours previously spent walking for water, allowing children to attend school regularly.

Every dollar donated goes directly toward the drilling equipment, materials, and community training to ensure the long-term maintenance of the pumps. Together, we can create a ripple effect of health and opportunity.
